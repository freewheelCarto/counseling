Virginia Arrington, LPC

Virginia knows how difficult life can be at times, whether mood related, issues within a relationship or an overwhelming circumstance. She desires to listen empathetically to each client, fostering hope within each session and together, creating a roadmap out of the problem. She brings an interpersonal style to therapy, meeting each individual where they are and providing a safe, nurturing environment to process their story. Her goal is for each client to feel empowered to make the necessary changes to lead a more purposeful and fulfilling life.

Virginia holds an Education Specialist degree in Marriage, Couples, & Family Counseling from the University of South Carolina and is a Licensed Professional Counselor. She is also a Nationally Certified Counselor.
