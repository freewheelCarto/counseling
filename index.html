Virginia Arrington, LPC

Virginia knows how difficult life can be at times, whether mood related, issues within a relationship or an overwhelming circumstance. She desires to listen empathetically to each client, fostering hope within each session and together, creating a roadmap out of the problem.

She brings an interpersonal style to therapy, meeting each individual where they are and providing a safe, nurturing environment to process their story. Her goal is for each client to feel empowered to make the necessary changes to lead a more purposeful and fulfilling life. Virginia counsels from a Christian perspective and enjoys processing spiritual development at the clientâ€™s request.

Virginia holds an Education Specialist degree in Marriage, Couples, & Family Counseling from the University of South Carolina and is a Licensed Professional Counselor. She is also a Nationally Certified Counselor.

Rates

Virginia Arrington Counseling Services, LLC has a rate of $100 per 50 minute follow-up session. She has a rate of $115 for an initial session, which includes assessment and goal setting to create an appropriate and customized treatment plan. She accepts all credit cards, checks, or exact cash as options for payment.

Insurance

Virginia Arrington Counseling Services, LLC is not in network with insurance companies. Virginia is happy to provide clients with detailed documentation, called superbills, regarding counseling services rendered. Clients can then choose to submit their superbills to their appropriate insurance carrier for possible out of network reimbursement.
